Tamales

Take Me Back Home

Ingredients for Masa

  • Corn Masa or Masa Harina
  • Lard
  • Chicken Broth
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt

Ingredients for Filling

  • Shredded Chicken
  • Dried Red Chiles
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Spcices (cumin, dried oregano, salt, pepper)

Ingredients for Assembly

  • Corn husks(soaked until soft)

Steps

  1. Soak Corn Husks: Submerge husks in warm water until soft.
  2. Make the Masa: Mix masa harina with lard, chicken broth, baking powder, and salt until smooth.
  3. Prepare the Red Sauce: Blend rehydrated red chiles with tomatoes, onion, garlic, cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine Sauce and Masa: Stir a portion of the red sauce into the masa for extra flavor.
  5. Assemble Tamales: Spread masa on a corn husk, add shredded chicken, and drizzle extra red sauce on top.
  6. Fold and Wrap: Fold the husk to secure the filling inside.
  7. Steam: Place tamales in a steamer and cook until the masa is firm and fully cooked.
