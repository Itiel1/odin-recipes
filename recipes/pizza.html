Pizza

Take Me Back Home

Ingredients for Dough

  • All-purpose Flour (3 Cups)
  • Warm Water (1 Cup)
  • Active Dry Yeast (2 Teaspoons)
  • Sugar (1 Teaspoon)
  • Salt (1 Teaspoon)
  • Olive Oil (2 Tablespoon)

Ingredients for Tomato Sauce

  • Tomato Sauce (14oz)
  • Garlic (1-2 Cloves)
  • Dried Oregano (1 Teaspoon)
  • Dried basil(1 Teaspoon)
  • Salt & Pepper (To Taste)

Ingredients for Toppings

  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (2 Cups)
  • Pepperoni Slices (20-30 Slices)
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (Optional)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves (Optional)

Ingredients for Assembly

  • Exta Olive Oil (For Brushing The Crust)

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C)
  2. Prepare the dough by mixing and kneading flour, warm water, yeast, sugar, salt, and olive oil; then let it rise and roll it out.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella, and arrange pepperoni slices on top.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil (if using) and serve.
